Technical Specifications and Features

Advanced Material Construction

The technical specifications of UNICEF High Performance tents represent years of research and development. The outer liner consists of 250 g/m² HDPE/LDPE laminated woven fabric, providing exceptional waterproofing with a 5000mm rating. This material choice ensures long-term durability in harsh environments while maintaining breathability for occupant comfort.

The frame structure utilizes lightweight yet robust aluminum pipes with steel connectors, creating a perfect balance between portability and strength. During my installations, I've consistently been impressed by how quickly these frames assemble, even in challenging conditions. The aluminum construction significantly reduces overall weight while maintaining structural integrity under extreme loads.

One of the most impressive features of UNICEF High Performance tents is their comprehensive ventilation system.
